Background

Sports gave me instinct.
Business gave me method.

I graduated from Paichai University (2022) with a double major in Leisure Sports and Business Administration. Sport taught me how people emotionally connect with brands. Business gave me the vocabulary to turn that into strategy.

At V WORKS (VMARK), a Daejeon-based ad agency, I build Meta and Naver campaign proposals for food franchise clients, run the ads, track weekly performance, and write monthly reports that explain why the numbers moved — not just what they were.

Currently enrolled in a Co-op Digital Marketing Program at Cornerstone College, Vancouver — refining my approach to SEO, content strategy, SMART goal-setting, buyer personas, and brand architecture, all in an English-language environment.
